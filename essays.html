Essay 3

I feel like if I'm going to be making more essays on blackchain, I should explain what it is first. Blockchain is a digital storage that stores data, whether that be cryptocurrency payments or NFT records. Blockchain is named for the "blocks" of data in a "chain". Blockchain is totally decentralized, in that it doesn't have a central bank or record of the chain, but instead copies of the chain are spread across multiple computers, called nodes. Since all nodes must have identical records, they all confirm each new bit of dada as it's added, making blockchain a highly secure way to store information. A single centralized system can be troubled by a single person making a mistake, and thus messing up the whole record without knowing. Blockchain can also be used for DeFi (Decentralized finance), storing contracts. When a node verifies data and sends it through to the chain, it is rewarded with a certain amount of the blockchains native currency. The most common use of blockchain is to log cryptocurrency transactions, such as Bitcoin. Blockchain is not without disadvantages, such as higher energy use, and lower transactions per second when logging currency payments or transfers. You can also have the data in your chain leaked if an attacker gets hold of your passkey (If the chain is so guarded). Finally, since blockchain is so secretive and secure, it is very appealing to criminals, making their illegal transactions harder to track.

Essay 4

According to one study, roughly a third of businesses believe that artificial intelligence will be one of the most dangerous cyber security threats in the coming years. ChatGPT being accessable by literally anybody, as well as being able to write code for you, for completely free no less, is believed by some to be able to write extremely advanced malware for attackers. Many are also worried about the very small reserve of individuals with the skills to combat AI based malware. The vice president of technology at Fastly, Sean Leach, states that AI will be used as a tool to assist in the creation of simple code, but won't be used as a way to make "super malware" for a while yet. Businesses are also concerned about their data being leaked by AI, or for AI, in the case of data being used as training data for machine learning models without the consent of the owner of the data. Some say that this could result in the data being revealed to the world through the use of the model. On the other hand, AI could also be used to significantly reduce the amount of work a single human security worker has to do, possibly tightening security around these companies data. One of the reasons many junior security staff don't stay there very long is the amount of tedious and repetitive work they have to do. In summary, as far as cyber security goes, businesses will have a lot more ground to cover in terms of protecting their data from leaks and AI based attacks. However they now also have a variety of AI based tools to assist them in combating such threats.

Essay 5

What is bullying? Bullying is repeated abuse in a physical, phycological, or relational manner. Bullying can lead to a variety of harmful consequences to both the bully and bullied, such as a higher likelyhood of developing a mental illnesses in the future. Bullies are often abused or mistreated at home, and sometimes need the same help a victim of bullying might. They may simply want to get out their anger or negative feelings on other individuals. Sometimes they may bully because they are being bullied themselves. There are many ways to deal with being bullied, though there is no real correct or foolproof way to stop the bullying. You can try to laugh off insults or go to a teacher, but a generally good thing to do is to show the bully they can't control you or your emotions. Above all don't let the words bother you, and instead focus on the brighter sides of your life. Hang out with friends so as to not be alone. Always report the bully to a trusted adult, openly or privately.
